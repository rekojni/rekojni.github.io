Offshore Account Opening Automation

Overview

Transformed manual offshore account opening from 1-2 hours to ~20 minutes per application. Created interactive PDF solution that auto-populates client data across multiple forms, eliminating repetitive entry and reducing errors.

The Challenge

Manual offshore account opening process was inefficient and error-prone:

  • Time-intensive: 1-2 hours per application impacting RM productivity
  • Repetitive data entry: Same client details manually entered across multiple forms
  • Error-prone: Manual transcription led to inconsistencies and delays
  • Poor experience: Tedious process for both staff and clients

The Solution

Developed interactive PDF using Adobe Acrobat Pro with "master input" system. Data entered once automatically populates across all forms through field mapping.

Key Innovation: Single data entry point with automatic field population across entire form package.

Results & Impact

Quantifiable Results

  • 70% time reduction per application
  • Full 5/5 rating from user surveys
  • Team-wide adoption across offshore unit

Process Benefits

  • Eliminated repetitive data entry
  • Reduced transcription errors
  • Improved client onboarding experience

Skills Demonstrated

  • Process optimisation: Identifying bottlenecks and designing solutions
  • Technical innovation: Adobe Acrobat Pro form development
  • Change leadership: Driving adoption through organic word-of-mouth
  • User-centric design: Creating intuitive tools for non-technical users
